User Guide

📱 The 5 Tabs

The application is organized into 5 main tabs:

  • Summary - Monthly overview: start, income, expenses, end
  • Base - Shared budget (common expenses with percentage)
  • Perso - Your personal income and expenses
  • Visa - All transactions paid by Visa
  • Marge - Credit line management

📋 Summary

The Summary tab displays an overview of your month:

  • Start - Balance at the beginning of the month (calculated automatically)
  • Income - Total Personal income for the month
  • Expenses - Account payable + Visa + Personal expenses
  • End - Expected balance at the end of the month
  • Account Payable - Amount to transfer for Base budget
  • Visa Payable - Total Visa purchases for the month
  • Credit Line Balance - Amount owed on credit line

🏠 Base Budget

The Base section is designed for expenses shared between partners:

  • Total Cost - The total amount of the expense
  • Percentage - Your contribution share (e.g., 50%)
  • Contribution - Automatically calculated (Cost x %)
  • Budget - Check to mark as planned amount (vs actual)

The header shows "Account Payable" (budget - actual) and "Deductions" (actual expenses).

💵 Personal Budget

The Perso section displays:

  • Start - Balance at the beginning of the month (editable for the first month)
  • Income - Salary, bonus, allowances, etc.
  • Expenses - Your personal expenses (non-Visa)

Ending balance is calculated: Start + Income - (Account Payable + Visa + Expenses).

💳 Visa Tab

The Visa tab groups all transactions paid by Visa:

  • Previous - Visa balance from previous month (editable)
  • Monthly Total - Sum of Visa purchases this month
  • Balance - Total to pay (previous + month)

Visa transactions can come from Base or Perso.

🏦 Credit Line

Manage your credit line:

  • Limit - Your credit limit (editable)
  • Used - Amount currently borrowed
  • Available - Remaining available credit
  • Borrowing - Add a withdrawal from the line
  • Repayment - Record a payment

🔄 Recurring Transactions

For monthly expenses (rent, subscriptions, etc.):

  • Enable the Recurring toggle when creating
  • The transaction will automatically appear each month
  • Skip a month: Disable the Recurring toggle in the month to skip
  • The app ends the recurrence at the previous month and creates a new one the following month

📊 Budget Tracking

Compare your planned budget vs actual expenses:

  • Accessible via the "Tracking" button in the menu
  • Shows by category: budgeted, spent, remaining
  • Color-coded progress bar (green/red)
  • Budget usage percentage

📱 Multi-device

MyBudget automatically syncs your data via iCloud:

  • Sign in to the same iCloud account on all your devices
  • Enable iCloud for the app in Settings > [Your name] > iCloud
  • On iOS: pull down to force sync
  • On macOS: use Cmd+R to sync manually

📤 Export and Import

Manage your data with export/import options:

  • Export JSON - Save all your transactions
  • Import JSON - Restore with duplicate detection
  • Export Adhoc CSV - Export Adhoc transactions
  • Import from email - Paste a formatted email

📧 Email Sharing

To send a transaction by email:

  1. Long press on a transaction (or right-click on Mac)
  2. Select "Send by email"
  3. The email will be pre-filled with the details

Email import format:

DATE: 2026-03-15
SECTION: Base
TYPE: Expense
CATEGORY: Groceries
AMOUNT: 150.00
RECURRING: No
NOTE: Weekly groceries

Frequently Asked Questions

How does "Account Payable" work in the Summary?

Account Payable = Base Budget - Actual Base Expenses + Adhoc. This is the net amount you need to transfer to the shared account.

How do I skip a month for a recurring transaction?

Go to the month you want to skip, open the recurring transaction and disable the "Recurring" toggle. The app will end the recurrence at the previous month and create a new one starting the following month.

What's the difference between Budget and non-Budget in Base?

"Budget" transactions represent your forecasts (planned amounts). Non-Budget transactions are actual expenses. Budget tracking compares the two.

Can I enter negative amounts?

Yes, negative amounts are supported for refunds, credits or adjustments.

How do I modify the starting balance?

In the Perso tab, tap on the "Start" amount to modify it. This is useful for the first month of use.

Does data sync between iPhone and Mac?

Yes, via iCloud. Make sure you're signed in to the same iCloud account and sync is enabled.

How does the credit line work?

Set your limit and initial balance. Borrowings increase the used balance, repayments reduce it. Available = Limit - Used.
