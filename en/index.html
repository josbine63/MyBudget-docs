User Guide

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Launch the MyBudget app
  2. Choose the Commun (Shared) or Perso (Personal) section
  3. Tap + to add your first transaction
  4. Select the type (Income or Expense) and category
  5. Enter the amount and save

👫 Shared Budget

The Commun section is designed for expenses shared between partners:

  • Total Cost - The total amount of the expense
  • Percentage - Your contribution share (e.g., 50%)
  • Contribution - Automatically calculated (Cost × %)

The shared budget balance is automatically carried over to your Personal section as an expense.

💵 Personal Budget

The Perso section displays:

  • Your personal income (Salary, Bonus, etc.)
  • Your personal expenses
  • Your contribution to the shared budget (automatic)
  • Available balance after all expenses

🔄 Recurring Transactions

For monthly recurring expenses (rent, subscriptions, etc.):

  1. Swipe a transaction to the right
  2. Or use the context menu (long press)
  3. Select Duplicate to next month

The transaction will be copied with the same date to the next month.

📱 Multi-device

MyBudget automatically syncs your data via iCloud:

  • Make sure you're signed in to the same iCloud account on all your devices
  • Enable iCloud for the app in Settings > [Your name] > iCloud
  • Changes sync within seconds

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the shared budget calculation work in Personal?

In the Personal section, shared budget expenses and the shared balance are automatically added as expenses. This represents your total contribution to the shared budget.

Can I enter negative amounts?

Yes, negative amounts are supported for refunds, credits or adjustments. Simply use the minus sign (-) before the amount.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is stored in your private iCloud database. It's encrypted and only accessible by devices connected to your Apple account.

Can I share my budget with my partner?

Currently, each user has their own database. For a shared budget, you can each enter your respective contributions with your participation percentage.

How do I edit a transaction?

Tap on a transaction in the list to open the edit screen. You can also use the context menu (long press) and select "Edit".

Does data sync between iPhone and Mac?

Yes, as long as you're signed in to the same iCloud account and sync is enabled, your data is available on iPhone, iPad and Mac.
